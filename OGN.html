OGN FAQs

Is NSFW Allowed?

Being a civilized server, we do not promote the act of NSFW. NSFW speeches may be made but should be kept to minimum, as well as spoiler tagged. NSFW Images are only allowed in a dedicated NSFW Channel (Coming Soon!), which will only be accessible by verified users of Age 18+.

 What Is The Purpose Of The Server?

As a Social Server, its primary focus is to enable meaningful social interactions to take place, without any form of disturbance or harassment. That being said, we punish Rule Breakers and even ban them should they continue to violate our Rules.

 Server Activeness?

Well, the majority of our Members are from the United States, the Minority being Asians. As such, the Server is much more active at Night (For the Asians) & the Morning (For Americans).

How Do I Become A Staff?

In any of our Server Channels, type /apply to fill in a HR Application Form. We review a batch of applicants every week. This is to ensure our Staffs are active, as inactive Staffs will be demoted.
