Paraborg Alpha v0.0.1 release?!

Have been anticipating for Paraborg's release? Today is the day!

Introducing Paraborg, a revolutionary bot that will enhance your Discord Server experience! As the developer of Paraborg, I look forward to new ideas and suggestions which I can explore and hopefully implement into Paraborg!

Paraborg was initially created as a private bot to aid me in mass-unbanning all globally "blacklisted" accounts (1400 accounts), which were previously banned by a bot which was later discontinued. With the use of the !unbanall command, the bot unbanned all 1.4k banned accounts under 5 minutes, which is actually significantly faster compared to the time a human user would take. The bot was supposed to be a one-time thing, but eventually I realized the assortment of things I could possible develop and do with the bot. Coding and developing the bot has since then became my new hobby.

Quality is our ultmost priority

Unlike most bot developers, I prioritize two things - Bot functionality and code-optimization over the bot's popularity. My belief is that if the bot is a quality one, people would be more obligated to use the services it offer. I also seek to improve on pre-existing ideas out there, based on identified user needs or feedbacks. Hence I look forward to continue receiving feedbacks from its users, as they helped shaped Paraborg into what it is today, and will definitely aid the bot in its future developments.
